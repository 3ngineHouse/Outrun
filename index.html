Outrun

We always thought our machines would turn on us. In the end, the power they gave us and the jealously that bought with it made us turn on each other. Now a handful look out at a world in ruins, while the rest struggle to survive in it


You have pushed your luck too far scavenging in the city ruins. A swarm of hunter-killer drones has spotted you. Luckily, your hard-won car carries the only weapon able to answer this threat: an EMP pulse generator. You just need keep ahead of the swarm long enough for it to charge.


Press 'a' to steer your car towards the top of the screen. Press 'd' to steer it towards the bottom. Avoid the wrecks on the ruined freeway - striking one will damage your car, striking too many will destroy it. If you can survive long enough for your EMP to charge, it will automatically discharge and destroy the swarm, leaving you victorious...for now.
